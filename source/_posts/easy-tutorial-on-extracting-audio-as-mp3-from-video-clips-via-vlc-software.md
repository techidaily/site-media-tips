## How to Convert to MP3 with VLC?

Step 1\. Download VLC and launch it

Step 2\. Click "Media" tab and choose "Convert/Save" in the drop-down menu.

You will be directed to a File Selection window. 

[](https://wiki.videolan.org/VLC%5FFeatures%5FFormats/)

![VLC Convert MP3](https://www.5kplayer.com/vlc/img/vlc-convert.jpg) 

Step 3\. Click the Add button to browse and choose a video file.

Step 4\. Click Convert/Save button to go to the convert window.

![VLC MP3 Convert](https://www.5kplayer.com/vlc/img/vlc-convert-add.jpg) 

Step 5\. Click on the Profile drop-down menu and select Audio-MP3 

Step 6\. Click on browse 

**VLC MP3 player can play media encoded with whatever codec:** Please refer to this site to see: <https://wiki.videolan.org/VLC%5FFeatures%5FFormats/>

![VLC Convert MP3](https://www.5kplayer.com/vlc/img/vlc-mp3.jpg)
